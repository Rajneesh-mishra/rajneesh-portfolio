About Me

As a Fullstack Gen AI and Agentic AI Developer, I design and implement intelligent systems that combine the power of large language models with autonomous agents capable of reasoning, planning, and executing complex tasks.

My work involves building RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation) systems that enable LLMs to access real-time data, creating multi-agent systems that collaborate to solve problems, and developing full-stack applications that seamlessly integrate AI capabilities.

I work with technologies like LangChain, LangGraph, OpenAI, Anthropic Claude, vector databases (Pinecone, Weaviate, Chroma), and modern frameworks to build production-ready AI applications.
